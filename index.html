Our Most Recent Game: REGROWN

Regrown is a 2D platformer, set in a world where humans once lived but have since disappeared. You play as a decommissioned search-and-rescue robot which unexpectedly reboots in the overgrown ruins of humanity, who must make use of both synthetic, upgrades of its chassis as well as symbiotic bonds it forms with the wildlife in order to survive.
